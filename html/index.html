Welcome to the What's Left Farm!

We are a wedding and events venue located just outside of Nashville, TN. Our main event space is a former horse barn, and through extensive renovations it has retained its rustic charm. The atmosphere lends itself to comfort for any visitor - even if on first thought, a barn does not sound like the space for you, we recommend you still come visit! You might be surprised at just how well it works for you.

With a capacity of 200 and a variety of event spaces to choose from, we have something for everyone. Call today at (662) 721-8155 to schedule a tour!

Who We Are

We are located on five acres of land originally part of the historic Curtis family farm, dating back to 1861. After winning Grand Champion Mule at the state fair in 1997, Martin Curtis, JR was asked the name of the farm he represented. With the promise that this land would not be developed, he named it the “What’s Left Farm.”

What We Do

The barn is 6400 square feet with gravel floors, large door openings at either end, and nine double doors along each side. It is outfitted with lights and electricity, but limited climate control so the expected season is March to November. We are a perfect space for rustic weddings, family reunions, and any number of social gatherings or photo shoots.

Where We Are

Hendersonville is the “City by the Lake”, and our property does indeed have a wonderful view of Old Hickory Lake from our situation atop a hill just across the street from the water! Even nestled in the peace of this town and the lake, we are only a 30-minutes drive from all the excitement of the city of Nashville. Numerous businesses in our area rent and deliver tables, chairs, and dance floors. Several excellent caterers and photographers are also convenient to the neighborhood.

Bonnie Hester, 2018.
